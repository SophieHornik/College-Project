OUR MISSION:

The mission of The University of Texas at Austin is to achieve excellence in the interrelated areas of undergraduate education, graduate education, research and public service. The university provides superior and comprehensive educational opportunities at the baccalaureate through doctoral and special professional educational levels.
The university contributes to the advancement of society through research, creative activity, scholarly inquiry and the development and dissemination of new knowledge, including the commercialization of University discoveries. The university preserves and promotes the arts, benefits the states economy, serves the citizens through public programs and provides other public service.

UT Athletics

Sports:

The University of Texas at Austin (UT Austin) boasts a rich and competitive sports program, known as the Texas Longhorns, and they are now members of the Southeastern Conference (SEC). UT Austin offers a wide range of sports, including football, basketball, baseball, softball, volleyball, swimming and diving, track and field, golf, soccer, and tennis. The football team is iconic, with multiple national championships and a massive fan base, playing home games at the Darrell K Royal-Texas Memorial Stadium. UT’s men’s and women’s basketball teams are also consistently strong, with regular appearances in NCAA tournaments. The baseball team has one of the most successful programs in NCAA history, boasting several College World Series titles. Additionally, their swimming and diving teams are powerhouse programs, with the men’s team consistently ranked among the best in the country, winning numerous national titles. As part of the highly competitive SEC, UT Austin’s sports teams continue their tradition of excellence, competing at the highest national levels across a wide variety of sports.

Student Life

Life at UT:

The University of Texas at Austin offers a vibrant student life, with over 1,300 student organizations and clubs catering to diverse interests, from academic and professional societies to recreational, cultural, and service-based groups. Whether you're into tech, arts, politics, or outdoor activities, UT has something for everyone. Living in Austin, often ranked as one of the best cities for students, adds to the appeal. With its lively music scene, food culture, and outdoor spaces like Zilker Park and Lady Bird Lake, students can enjoy a balance of academic life and city living. Austin's progressive, inclusive atmosphere is reflected on campus, fostering a welcoming environment for all students. The combination of a supportive campus community, endless opportunities for involvement, and the dynamic city of Austin makes student life at UT Austin exciting and fulfilling.

Academic Programs

Programs:

The University of Texas at Austin is renowned for its wide array of academic programs, offering over 170 undergraduate majors and numerous graduate programs across 18 colleges and schools. UT Austin is especially known for its strong programs in fields such as business, engineering, computer science, law, and public affairs. The McCombs School of Business, Cockrell School of Engineering, and the College of Natural Sciences consistently rank among the top in the nation. UT Austin also excels in liberal arts, fine arts, communications, and social sciences, with highly regarded programs in journalism, film, and government. The university promotes interdisciplinary learning, research opportunities, and innovation, giving students access to cutting-edge facilities and world-class faculty. Whether you're interested in the arts, sciences, or emerging fields like data science and artificial intelligence, UT Austin provides an exceptional academic environment that fosters intellectual growth and prepares students for global leadership.
